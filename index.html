About Us

KRT Dance Studio (快来跳家族) is led by Zin Ricky, Negeri Sembilan PTMM Chairperson. We specialize in Zumba, Square Dance, and Kpop for kids & teens. Located at 20a, Jalan Prima 4, 71010 Port Dickson, our studio is a vibrant hub for dance, fitness, and community joy.

Our Classes

  • Zumba (Morning & Evening sessions)
  • Square Dance (Fun & beginner-friendly)
  • Kpop Kids & Teens (Modern choreography + social energy)

Weekly Schedule

Monday to Friday: Morning Zumba with Zin Ricky
Tuesday & Thursday Evening: Instructor-led Zumba
Friday Evening: Zumba with Ricky
Saturday: Kids & Teens Kpop Classes
Sunday: FitDance or Legacy Day
